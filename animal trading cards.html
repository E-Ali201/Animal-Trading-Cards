Tiger

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is one of the largest extant cat species and a member of the genus Panthera

  • scientific Name:Panthera tigris
  • Average Length:from 250-390 cm
  • Family:Felidae
  • Existence:1.2 million years

It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange-brown fur with a lighter underside. It is an apex predator, primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and wild boar.

Characteristics:
The tiger has a muscular body with powerful forelimbs, a large head and a tail that is about half the length of its body!
